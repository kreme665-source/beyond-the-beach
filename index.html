The Problem

Travel tools answer the wrong question.

📍 "What are the top 10 things to do here?"
⭐ "What has the highest rating?"
📸 "What should I post on Instagram?"
"What kind of experience are you actually looking for?"

Some travelers want food. Some want music and nightlife. Some want quiet beauty. Some want culture and local connection. Some want adventure. Travel should understand that — and most platforms don't.
